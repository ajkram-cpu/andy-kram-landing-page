# Andy Kram Landing Page

This project is a personal professional landing page built for BAIS 3300 (Digital Product Management) at the University of Iowa. It showcases my background, skills, and project experience in a clean, recruiter-friendly format that goes beyond a traditional resume.

The goal of this site is to provide a quick, structured overview of who I am and what I bring within 30–60 seconds for recruiters and hiring managers.

---

## Features

- Professional hero section with name, tagline, and headshot
- About/Bio section with academic and career information
- Skills section organized by category
- Featured projects with tools and descriptions
- Contact section with LinkedIn, GitHub, and email
- Fully responsive design
- Accessible and semantic HTML structure
